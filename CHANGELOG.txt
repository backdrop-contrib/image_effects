Imagecache Actions 7.x-1.0 2012-xx-xx
-------------------------------------
No changelog exists from before this 7.x-1.0 version. So the changelog starts
from here. 7.x-1.0 is the first reasonably well working D7 version. The 7.x-0.0
version was based on posted patches in the D7 port request issue and nver well
tested. This 7.x-1.0 version is also not extensively tested by the maintainers,
but has been used in many (live) sites - currently over 5.000 reported
7.x-1.x-dev installs - so has receieved fairly good "test coverage".
